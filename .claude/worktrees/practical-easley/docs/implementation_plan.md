# Wolf Pacc Audio — Implementation & Growth Plan

This document merges the **site/build todo list** (analytics, SEO, MailerLite, blog, etc.) with **conversion and growth priorities** below.

---

## Phase A — Site & infrastructure (original 15-item plan)

High level: GA4 + Meta Pixel on all pages; SEO alt text; trust badges; MailerLite universal + footer signup + welcome automation; Lemon Squeezy affiliates/upsells; blog scaffold; Discord + social footer; audio players when assets exist; Q3 product launches (melodies, beats, expansions) with checkout URLs.

Track execution status in your issue tracker or git; this file is the master checklist reference.

---

## Phase B — Strategic / conversion (add to the plan)

### 1. Homepage (biggest issue)

- Headline is likely unclear → needs a **direct outcome** (what the visitor gets).
- Add **one clear CTA above the fold** (e.g. Get Free Pack / Shop Now).
- **Show what you sell immediately** — VST, drum kits, melodies (not buried).
- Add **social proof** — artist names (e.g. Wiz Khalifa), placements, stats if any.

### 2. Free pack funnel (revenue leak)

After download, you need:

- **Redirect / thank-you page** (post-download).
- **Offer a paid product immediately** on that page.
- **Discount** (e.g. “Full kit for $9 — limited”).

Right now: no immediate monetization = lost sales.

### 3. Email automation (critical)

MailerLite should **not** only deliver the file. Minimum sequence:

- **Email 1:** Delivery + soft intro to the brand.
- **Email 2:** How to use the sounds in a DAW.
- **Email 3:** Push a paid product.
- **Email 4:** Urgency (discount ending).

Without this → leads do not convert.

### 4. Product pages (conversion)

Each product needs:

- **Audio previews** (multiple).
- **Clear description** — what’s inside (formats, counts).
- **Who it’s for** (genre, skill level).
- **Outcome** — what they can make with it.
- **Clean CTA** — Buy Now.

### 5. Offer structure (missing)

Build a **ladder**:

- **Entry** — $7–$19.
- **Main** — $29–$79.
- **Premium** — VST / bundle.

No clear ladder → weaker sales.

### 6. Traffic (if not posting consistently)

Aim for **daily content**:

- Beat videos, sound demos, cookups, before/after using your sounds.

**Platforms:** TikTok, Instagram, YouTube Shorts.

### 7. Branding consistency

Same **colors, fonts, and style** across:

- Website, pack art, socials.

Inconsistency → hurts trust.

### 8. Checkout optimization (Lemon Squeezy)

- **Product images** on checkout.
- **Short benefit bullets**.
- **Branded feel** — not generic checkout.

### 9. Retargeting (missed revenue)

If you are **not**:

- Running ads, and/or
- Capturing **returning visitors** (pixel audiences, email, etc.)

→ warm traffic is lost.

### 10. Urgency

Add **reason to buy now**:

- Discounts with deadlines.
- Limited offers.
- Bundles positioned as limited / best value.

No urgency → no reason to buy now.
