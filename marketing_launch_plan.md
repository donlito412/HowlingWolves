# Wolf Pacc Audio — 30-Day Marketing Launch Plan

## The Vision
Position Wolf Pacc Audio as a go-to brand for producers and artists — the next Cymatics or Native Instruments. This plan focuses on Instagram and Facebook to drive sales across all products (VSTs, beat packs, sample packs, bundles).

## Your Funnel

Traffic → Free Pack → Email List → Sales

1. Social posts drive traffic to wolfpaccaudio.com
2. Free Teaser Pack (email-gated) captures leads
3. MailerLite email sequence nurtures and sells
4. Lemon Squeezy handles purchases

Every post should ultimately point somewhere: free pack link, product page, or website.

## Posting Cadence

Instagram: 4-5x per week (mix of Reels, carousels, and static posts)
Facebook: 3-4x per week (repurpose IG content + join producer groups)
Stories: Daily — behind the scenes, polls, quick sound previews

Best posting times for music producers: evenings (7-10 PM) and weekends when people are in the studio.

## Week-by-Week Plan

### Week 1: Establish Presence & Drive Free Pack Downloads
- Post 3-4 value/demo posts showcasing your sounds
- Pin a "Free Pack" post to the top of your IG profile
- Add wolfpaccaudio.com/free-sounds/ as your link in bio
- Share 15-second sound previews in Reels and Stories
- Post in 3-5 Facebook producer groups (give value first, don't just spam links)

### Week 2: Build Social Proof & Engagement
- Share any testimonials, DMs, or user feedback you've received
- Create "before/after" content showing beats made with your sounds
- Run a poll in Stories: "What genre should the next pack be?"
- Engage heavily — comment on other producers' posts, reply to every DM
- Start a "Sound of the Day" or "Loop of the Day" Story series

### Week 3: Product Spotlight & Soft Selling
- Spotlight the Howling Wolves VST — show it in action
- Create a carousel breaking down what's in your bundles and the savings
- Share a full beat-making session using only Wolf Pacc sounds (Reel)
- Post a limited-time discount or bundle deal
- Send first sales email to your MailerLite list

### Week 4: Push Hard & Evaluate
- Run a flash sale (48-72 hours) — promote across all channels
- Create urgency posts: countdown Stories, "last chance" posts
- Send 2-3 emails during the sale period
- Review analytics: which posts got the most engagement and clicks?
- Double down on what worked, cut what didn't
